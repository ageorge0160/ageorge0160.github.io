I have been working on a personal site for a few months now, taking inspiration from some design templates and working to include the many projects and aspects of my life. I ultimately chose to create my site using Jekyll for the ease of hosting with Github. I also was looking to build a site that I could easily update as I continue to create new things.

Throughout the site I use my own photography and logo designs. I hope to add sections to the site in the future to showcase my photography and other skills.
